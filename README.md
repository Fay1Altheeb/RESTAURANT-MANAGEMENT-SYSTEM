# RESTAURANT-MANAGEMENT-SYSTEM
We are creating a database management system for Joe & The Juice restaurant. Our system will clarify and simplify the way everything works for the managers, keeping them updated and aware of the workflow for all branches of the restaurant. 

The system will represent managers and employees working in each branch, it will inform the manager about the branch’s states and tracks the number of orders for each branch by their locations, it’s also giving the ability to determine customer orders and the dishes they choose with the bills of the orders and their total prices
